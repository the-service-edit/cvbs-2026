How a Brief Moves Through the Team

One workflow · everyone aligned
1
Brief Lands
Enquiry comes in via the website form and drops straight into Airtable — tagged, timestamped, visible to everyone.
2
Owner Assigned
A team member picks it up or it's allocated from the dashboard. One owner, no overlap, no “I thought you had it.”
3
Proposal Sent
Open the shared template, drop in venue details, send. Every proposal goes out on-brand — no matter whose name is on it.
4
Full Visibility
Anyone on the team can open the record and see the full history. Cover, hand over, or jump in seamlessly.
