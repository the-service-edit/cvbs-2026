FAQ

Common questions

Do you source venues for small executive meetings?
Yes. Not every event is large. We regularly source private boardrooms and executive suites for small, high-stakes meetings where the space, the technology and the service matter more than the size. The process is the same, and it is still free to you.
Can you keep the arrangements discreet?
Yes. For board and executive meetings we keep details confidential and, if you prefer, approach venues without naming your organisation until you are ready. We manage the arrangements quietly so your team can focus on the meeting.
