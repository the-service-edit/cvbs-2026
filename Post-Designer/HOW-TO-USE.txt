CVBS POST DESIGNER

Open CVBS-Post-Designer.html in your browser (nothing to install, works offline).

TEMPLATES
 Cover pages: Offer, Site visit, Event, Educational, Positioning, Destination,
   Offers roundup, Client result.
 Inner pages (marked ›): Content page, Numbered point, Quote, Call to action.
 Build a carousel by making the cover, then each inner page in turn, and
 downloading them in order (slide 1, 2, 3...).

CONTROLS
 Format: Instagram 4:5, Square, or LinkedIn wide.
 Align:  Left (photo/info pages) or Centre (statement pages).
 Photo:  pick a starter or Upload your own. POS moves it up/down,
         DIM darkens it so text stays readable on bright photos.
 Content: type your words. Wrap a word in *asterisks* for teal.
          Press Enter in the headline to control line breaks.

Download PNG saves a finished 2160px image to your Downloads.
