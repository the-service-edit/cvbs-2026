CVBS POST DESIGNER  (carousel edition)

Open CVBS-Post-Designer.html in your browser. Nothing to install, works offline.

BUILD A CAROUSEL
 1. The "Slides in this carousel" strip shows your slides. Click + to add one,
    click a slide to edit it. Duplicate / Delete / Move ◀ ▶ reorder them.
 2. For each slide pick a Template and fill the Content fields.
    Cover pages: Offer, Site visit, Event, Educational, Positioning,
    Destination, Offers roundup, Client result.
    Inner pages (marked ›): Content page, Numbered point, Quote, Call to action.
 3. Format (Instagram 4:5 / Square / LinkedIn wide) applies to the whole set.
 4. Slide 1 shows "Swipe"; later slides get progress dots automatically.

PER SLIDE
 Align Left or Centre. Add a photo (starter or Upload), POS moves it,
 DIM darkens it so text stays readable. Wrap a word in *asterisks* for teal.

EXPORT
 "Download carousel (.zip)"  = every slide as a numbered PNG, ready to post.
 "Download this slide only"  = just the current slide.
 On Instagram, upload the PNGs in order as a carousel.
