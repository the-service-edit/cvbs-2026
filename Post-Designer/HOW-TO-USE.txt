CVBS POST DESIGNER

1. Double-click CVBS-Post-Designer.html to open it in your web browser
   (Chrome, Safari or Edge). Nothing to install. Works offline.

2. Pick a Template (Offer, Site visit, Event, etc.) and a Format
   (Instagram 4:5, Square, or LinkedIn wide).

3. Add a photo: click one of the starter photos, or "Upload photo" to use
   your own. Use POS to slide the photo up/down and DIM to darken it for
   readable text.

4. Type your words in the Content fields. Wrap a word in *asterisks* to make
   it teal. Press Enter in the headline to control line breaks.

5. Tick "LinkedIn footer" for the company-name signature instead of the
   website and Swipe.

6. Click "Download PNG". The finished image saves to your Downloads,
   ready to post.

Everything is your CVBS brand: navy, teal, stone and Inter.
