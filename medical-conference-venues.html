Medical and healthcare conference venues

Scientific programs, exhibitions and CPD. We source medical and healthcare conference venues built for the science and the scale.

Medical and healthcare conferences are among the most complex events to run: large scientific programs, concurrent sessions, trade exhibitions, poster space, and accreditation or CPD requirements. We source venues with the capacity and the technical capability to support them, then coordinate accommodation and logistics around the program.

What we handle

  • Plenary, concurrent session and poster space
  • Trade exhibition floor for sponsors and suppliers
  • Reliable AV and streaming for hybrid delegates
  • Room blocks for interstate and international delegates

FAQ

Common questions

Can you source venues for large medical conferences with exhibitions?
Yes. We regularly source for events that combine a scientific program with a trade exhibition and poster space. We confirm the venue can run concurrent sessions, host exhibitors and manage delegate flow, then negotiate the commercials across the whole event.
Can you arrange accommodation for interstate and international delegates?
Yes. We negotiate and manage room blocks alongside the venue, often as a single arrangement, which usually improves the rate on both. We handle rooming lists and changes so your team can focus on the program.
