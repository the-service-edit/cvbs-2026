# CVBS — event photography shot list

These are the photos that will take the website from "very good" to genuinely best-in-class. The site currently shows beautiful but empty venue rooms; what it's missing is people and events in action. Capture these at upcoming events (or pull from past ones), and we'll thread them through the homepage and every service page.

## What we need most (priority order)

1. **Keynote / plenary in session** — a full or near-full room from the back or side, stage lit, speaker mid-presentation, audience facing forward. This is the single most valuable shot.
2. **Networking / reception** — people standing, talking, drinks in hand, warm light. Candid, not posed. Shows energy and connection.
3. **Gala / awards dinner** — round tables dressed, room glowing, guests seated. Sells the premium end of what you source.
4. **Breakout / workshop** — smaller group, cabaret or boardroom setup, people engaged, laptops or notes out.
5. **Registration / arrival** — the welcome desk, lanyards, a branded moment. Sets the "well-run event" tone.

## Nice to have

- A delegate close-up: one or two people listening intently, or laughing at a table.
- Catering detail: a beautifully plated course or a styled coffee/canapé station.
- A wide exterior or foyer shot of a venue with people moving through it.
- **Karen and Anthony at work** — even one natural shot of them on-site or on a call. Real faces build more trust than any stock image.

## Technical notes (give these to the photographer)

- **Landscape orientation**, high resolution (shoot RAW / largest JPEG). We crop to wide and square, so leave breathing room around the subject.
- **Candid over posed.** Real moments, real expressions. Avoid stiff line-ups and peace signs.
- **Warm, natural light** where possible. Avoid harsh flash.
- **People as the hero**, the room as the backdrop. The opposite of what we have now.
- A spread of **horizontal and a few vertical** frames gives us the most flexibility.
- **Get permission / model releases** for anyone clearly identifiable, so we can use the images freely on the site and in marketing.

## Where each goes

- Keynote, networking, gala → the "events" band on each service page (currently placeholders) and a new experience strip on the homepage.
- Karen and Anthony → the homepage "the people" section and the About page.
- Catering / detail shots → supporting imagery throughout.

Six to eight strong images is enough to transform the whole site. Send them over and I'll do the rest.
