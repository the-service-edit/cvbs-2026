Home/How we're paid
Transparency

Free to you. Here's exactly why.

The most common question we get is “what's the catch?” There isn't one, but you deserve to understand the model before you trust us with your event.

Start your brief

Venues pay a standard commission on bookings, to travel agents, to event planners, to anyone who brings them business. That commission exists whether or not you use CVBS.

When you book through us, the venue pays that commission to CVBS instead of keeping it. You pay the venue's rate, usually a better rate than you'd get yourself, because we negotiate it, and you get our sourcing, negotiating and management at no charge. The venue fills the room. Everyone is better off.

Why this keeps us honest

Because commissions are broadly similar across properties, we gain nothing by steering you toward one venue over another. The only thing that grows our business is getting your event right so you come back. That aligns us with you, not with any single hotel.

FAQ

Straight answers

If it's free, what's the catch?
There isn't one. Venues pay a commission on bookings whether they come through an agent or not. When you book through CVBS, that commission comes to us, so you get expert sourcing and negotiation at no extra cost, and the venue fills a room it wanted to fill.
Won't you just push the venue that pays you most?
No. Commissions are broadly similar across venues, so we have no incentive to favour one over another. Our reputation, and our repeat clients, depend on recommending what genuinely fits your brief.
Could I get a better rate booking direct?
Rarely. Our negotiated rates and inclusions usually beat public pricing, and you avoid the time cost of doing it yourself. If booking direct were better for you, we'd tell you.
