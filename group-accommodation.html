FAQ

Common questions

Is there a minimum group size?
Group rates typically apply from around 10 rooms per night, but talk to us, we'll always check what's achievable for your numbers.
Can you arrange accommodation alongside a conference?
Yes. We regularly package delegate accommodation with the conference venue as a single negotiation, which strengthens your rate on both.
What does it cost?
Nothing to you. We're paid by the property, so our help with group accommodation is free.
