The process

From brief to booked, without the busywork.

You stay in control of the decision. We take on everything that makes venue sourcing slow, and it costs you nothing.

Start my search

You send one brief

Dates, delegate or guest numbers, location, budget and any must-haves. Use the two-minute form or talk it through with us, whichever's faster for you. One conversation replaces dozens of enquiries.

We source and negotiate

We tap 35 years of relationships to find matching venues, including spaces that never appear on booking sites, confirm real availability, and negotiate rates and inclusions on your behalf.

You compare a clear shortlist

Within 48 hours you get a like-for-like shortlist: rooms, rates, catering, AV and accommodation, side by side. No spreadsheets to build, no quotes to chase.

We confirm and stay on call

Once you choose, we lock the rate, manage the contract detail and remain your point of contact through to the event, and beyond, for the next one.

FAQ

Common questions

Is CVBS really free?
Yes. We're paid a standard commission by the venue you book, the same commission the venue would pay regardless. So our service is free to you, and our negotiating usually leaves you better off than booking direct.
How is that unbiased?
Because we're paid similar commissions across venues, we have no reason to push one property over another. We recommend what fits your brief, see how we're paid.
What do you need from me to start?
Just the basics: dates, numbers, location and budget. The more you tell us, the sharper the shortlist, but we can start with very little.
How fast is the shortlist, really?
Typically within 48 hours. Urgent brief? Tell us, relationships let us move quickly when it counts.
