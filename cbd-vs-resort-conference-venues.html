CBD vs resort conference venues

Should your conference be in the heart of the city or out at a resort? Both work well, for different events. Here is how to decide.

Delegate access and travel

CBD venues win on access: close to airports, public transport and accommodation, which lifts attendance and makes day delegates easy. Resort and regional venues ask delegates to travel, which suits residential conferences and retreats where being away from the office is the point.

Focus and experience

A resort or regional property keeps delegates together, which is ideal for culture-building, strategy offsites and incentives. A CBD hotel offers energy, dining and after-hours options on the doorstep, which suits larger conferences and external delegates.

Cost

City venues can carry higher room rates but lower travel and transfer costs. Resorts may offer competitive packages midweek and in shoulder seasons, but factor in transfers and the longer stay. The cheaper headline is not always the cheaper event.

Accommodation and logistics

Both usually offer rooms on site. Resorts simplify logistics by keeping everything in one place, while CBD events give delegates more accommodation choice nearby. Either way, bundling accommodation with the venue strengthens your rate.

How to choose

Match the venue to the outcome. Connection and focus point to a resort, while access and scale point to the CBD. We source both across Australia, in cities like Sydney and Melbourne and in wine country and island destinations. Tell us the brief and we will shortlist the right fit.
