# CVBS Instagram Carousels

Five reusable carousel templates, designed pixel-for-pixel to match the CVBS website (navy / teal / stone, Inter, the "since 1989 · free to you · 48-hour shortlist" story).

All five use a liquid-glass look (frosted panels over your venue photos), the same glass language already on your site in the frosted nav bar and hero badge. Templates 04 and 05 are the priority set, built for your two most common post types: offers/EDMs and site visits. Templates 01 to 03 cover education, positioning and proof.

Each carousel comes two ways:

- **PNGs** (1080 x 1350, in each folder) - ready to post straight to Instagram.
- **PDF** (one per folder) - drag it onto canva.com to import as a fully editable design. Text comes in live in Inter, so you can swap copy and reuse the layout for any future post.

## To edit in Canva
1. Go to canva.com, click **Create a design** then **Import file** (or just drag the PDF onto the homepage).
2. Each page becomes an editable slide. Click any text to rewrite it.
3. To post: Share, Download, PNG. Or repost the supplied PNGs as-is.

## The three templates

**01 - Budget mistakes** (7 slides). Save-bait listicle. Swap the five mistakes for any "5 things" angle: questions to ask a venue, signs you're overpaying, etc.

**02 - Searched vs Sourced** (6 slides). Your core positioning. The contrast layout works for any "the hard way vs the CVBS way" message.

**03 - Client result** (7 slides). Proof story: brief, challenge, what we did, the numbers, a quote, CTA. Drop in a real (de-identified) job each time.

**04 - Offer / EDM** (6 slides, liquid glass). For announcing a venue deal, the same content you send as an EDM. Filled with the real Kimpton Margot winter day-delegate package ($110pp, April to June 2026). Swap the photo, headline, price and inclusions for any offer. To re-skin: in Canva, replace the background image on each slide and edit the text.

**05 - Site visit** (6 slides, liquid glass). For "we went and saw it" posts. Filled with real Hyatt Place Essendon Fields specs (1,700+ sqm pillarless, up to 1,500 guests, 6 rooms, 166 keys, 10 min from Melbourne Airport). Swap the photo, venue name and the four numbers for any venue you visit.

## Notes before posting
- Template 03: the quote and figures are placeholders. Replace with a real, de-identified result.
- Templates 04 and 05: the offer and venue facts are pulled from your website and the venues' own listings, current as of build. Confirm the offer is still live and the specs are current before posting.
- Template 05 says "we checked out" the venue. Only post that framing if a CVBS site visit actually happened.
