Association conference venue sourcing

Multi-day association conferences with delegates, exhibitors and member budgets. We source venues that work for your members and for the books.

Association conferences carry pressures most corporate events do not: member budgets to respect, sponsors and exhibitors to accommodate, host cities that rotate each year, and a committee to keep informed. We source venues that balance the delegate experience with the commercial reality of a membership organisation, and we manage the moving parts so your committee does not have to.

What we handle

  • Plenary, breakout and exhibition space under one roof
  • Delegate accommodation at member-friendly rates
  • Rotating host cities across Australia, year on year
  • Budgets and contracts a board or committee can sign off

FAQ

Common questions

Can you help an association conference move cities each year?
Yes. Many associations rotate host cities to share the travel load across members. We source in every Australian capital and major regional centre, and because we know the venues in each, we keep the experience consistent while the location changes. Our destinations page shows where we work.
Can you work within an association's budget and committee process?
Yes. We are used to budgets that have to satisfy a board and members. We present clear comparisons that make the trade-offs easy to approve, and we negotiate rates and inclusions that protect the bottom line without cutting the delegate experience.
