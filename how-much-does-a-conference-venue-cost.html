How much does a conference venue cost in Australia?

There's no single answer, but there are clear ranges, and clear reasons one venue quotes far more than another. Here's how Australian conference pricing actually works.

Day-delegate packages (DDR)

Most conference hotels price per delegate, per day, bundling room hire, catering (arrival tea/coffee, morning tea, lunch, afternoon tea) and basic AV. In 2026, day-delegate rates commonly sit in a broad band depending on city, venue tier and group size, capital-city four- and five-star venues sit at the top, regional and suburban options lower. Always confirm exactly what's bundled.

Room hire and minimum spend

For functions, launches and dinners, venues often charge room hire plus a minimum food-and-beverage spend rather than a per-head package. The trade-off between the two is negotiable, and it's where a venue finder earns its keep.

The line items people forget

  • AV beyond the basics (technicians, screens, streaming)
  • Wi-Fi and bandwidth for large or hybrid events
  • Parking and valet
  • Service charges and public-holiday loadings
  • Accommodation for delegates and speakers

How to pay less without dropping quality

Rates move with season, day of week and how much business you bring. Midweek and shoulder-season dates are cheaper; bundling accommodation with the venue improves both rates. And venues quote agents better than the public, because they want the repeat business.
