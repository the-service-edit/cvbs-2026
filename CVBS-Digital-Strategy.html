5Getting found

Be the answer, not just a result.

How people search is changing fast. You need to win both ways.

SEO · found on Google

A clear page for each city and service, written the way people actually search. So you appear for "conference venues Sydney" without paying for ads, and the traffic compounds over time.

AEO · recommended by AI

More buyers now ask an assistant like ChatGPT or Google's AI "who can find me a venue in Brisbane?" Answer Engine Optimisation makes sure those tools name CVBS as the trusted answer.

  • ✓
    Why it matters nowA growing share of people get their shortlist from an assistant before they ever open a website. If you are not in that answer, you are not in the running.
  • ✓
    Your 35 years is the perfect raw materialThese tools reward exactly what you already have: clear answers, real results, named clients and genuine expertise. Most competitors have none of it.
  • ✓
    The simple first stepPlain, well-written city and service pages, plus a proper FAQ that answers the real questions buyers ask. Easy to start, and it builds on itself.
The shift

Search is splitting in two: Google and AI assistants. Win both, and you are the name that comes up however people look.

10Our philosophy

Technology should never replace relationships.

Your value has always come from people. The relationships you have built with venue managers and suppliers over 35 years are what make CVBS different. Nothing here changes that. Technology simply removes the repetitive admin and organises what you know, so your team has better tools and more time for what matters most.

The reassurance

We are not changing the heart of CVBS. We are protecting it, strengthening it, and making it easier to grow.
