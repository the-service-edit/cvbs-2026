The concierge process

From brief to booked, without the busywork.

01

You send one brief

Dates, numbers, location, budget. A two-minute form or a quick call. One conversation instead of fifty enquiries.

02

We source and negotiate

We check real availability across matching venues, including rooms that never show as free online, then negotiate rates and inclusions on your behalf.

03

You compare a shortlist

Within 48 hours you get a like-for-like shortlist: rates, inclusions, capacities and trade-offs side by side. No spreadsheets to build.

04

We confirm and coordinate

We lock the rate, handle the contract detail, and coordinate accommodation and suppliers through to the day. You stay in control throughout.
