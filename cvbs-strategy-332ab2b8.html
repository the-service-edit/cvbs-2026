  • 1
    It looks stuck in the pastThe site still links to Google+, a platform shut down in 2019, and runs on software from the same era. To a visitor it signals a business that hasn't moved on, the opposite of the truth.

The website is the first thing a client meets, before any of you. Right now it is your weakest salesperson.
