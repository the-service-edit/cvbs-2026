Our story

We run on relationships, not algorithms.

Conference Venues and Booking Services has been sourcing the right spaces for Australian organisations since 1989, quietly, independently, and at no cost to the people we work for.

Start my search

A booking site shows you what's listed. We know what's actually available, because we've spent more than three decades building trust with the general managers and sales directors who decide.

That trust is the whole business. It's how a venue that's "fully booked" finds you a room. It's how a standard rate becomes a better one. And it's why organisations keep coming back to us for the next event, and the one after that, we've held some of those relationships, on both sides, for over thirty years.

What we believe

  • Independence matters. We work for you, not for one hotel group. Our advice spans the whole market.
  • Free should mean free. We're paid by the venue, so our help costs you nothing, and we never trade your interests for a bigger commission.
  • One relationship, start to finish. The same person who takes your brief is there at the final invoice.
  • Knowledge compounds. Decades of rates and venue data sit behind every shortlist and every negotiation.
